# Marketing Funnel & Conversion Performance Analysis

## 📌 Task 3 | Future Interns | Data Science & Analytics Internship

## 📖 Overview
This project analyzes a real-world bank marketing campaign dataset to understand 
how customers move through the marketing funnel — from being contacted to 
subscribing to a term deposit. The goal is to identify conversion rates, 
drop-off points, and actionable insights to improve campaign performance.

## 🎯 Objective
- Analyze marketing funnel data to identify conversion drop-offs
- Measure lead-to-customer conversion rates
- Compare performance across job profiles and campaign months
- Provide actionable recommendations to improve conversions

## 📈 Key Insights
- **Total Customers Contacted:** 4,521
- **Total Subscriptions:** 521
- **Overall Conversion Rate:** 11.52%
- Management and Blue-collar professionals were contacted the most
- May and July had the highest campaign activity
- Only 11.52% of contacted customers subscribed — indicating significant drop-off

## 💡 Recommendations
1. Focus future campaigns on **retired and student** segments — they show higher conversion tendency
2. **May and July** are peak months — allocate more budget during these periods
3. Reduce calls to already-contacted customers to avoid campaign fatigue
4. Personalize outreach for **management professionals** who were most contacted but had low conversion
